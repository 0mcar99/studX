Frequently asked questions

Everything students ask before moving their learning stack to StudyShare AI.

Which file types are supported?

PDF, PPT/PPTX, DOC/DOCX, TXT, image formats, audio clips, and educational video files.

Yes. You can target personal uploads, saved resources, or selected marketplace documents.

How do token rewards work?

Tokens are earned from quality uploads, downloads, likes, and community activity streaks.

Is there role-based security?

User and admin roles are enforced through protected routes and audit trail visibility.

Can institutions use this platform?

Yes. Campus Team supports shared spaces, moderation controls, and org-level insights.

Do you support dark mode?

Yes. The interface auto-adapts to your system preference while preserving readability.
